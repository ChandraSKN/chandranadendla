What Most People Are Missing About Gen AI

Despite its popularity, there are five truths people often overlook:

  1. Generative AI doesn‚Äôt understand ‚Äî it predicts.
    It generates the most statistically probable next word, pixel, or sound ‚Äî not necessarily the most meaningful or correct one.

  2. Bias is baked in.
    AI reflects the data it was trained on. That includes human stereotypes, misinformation, and cultural biases ‚Äî amplified at scale.

  3. It can hallucinate facts.
    Especially in text and code, Gen AI can generate responses that sound plausible but are totally wrong.

  4. It‚Äôs not always legal.
    Many models are trained on copyrighted or proprietary data. This opens up grey areas in IP law, especially in art, code, and music.

  5. It‚Äôs energy-hungry.
    Training large models consumes immense computational power and energy ‚Äî raising sustainability concerns rarely discussed in AI hype.
